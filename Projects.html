Work Related

At WWT (World Wide Technology) myself and 6 other interns built a system for GET, (Global Engineering Team).

The ask was, "we have a significant need to gather, consolidate, and analyze feedback from customers, partners, and internal stakeholders".

We are asking you to build a system of collecting and analyzing this feedback from a variety of sources to include EBCs (Equity based Compensation) and CACs (Customer acquisition cost).

Over a 10 week period we put together an entire system, to first collect information, feedback, etc. from customers and parters after meetings, or at the end of the week. Then we would get that data into a spreadsheet and analyze it. Once we got it analyzed we would send it back to the party that needed it.
